About Us

Our History

When the founders came to the U.S. in 1988 in search of a better life, they found it difficult to adjust to a culture that had a great consumption of meat and fewer places that would suit their needs. This struggle sparked the birth of our restaurant, Roots of Asia- a place to bring comfort to people with similar experiences through food. Over the years, personal stories wove into a tapestry of life and emotions as one has had to leave loved ones behind, others found their way to stable ground, while others harnessed their strengths and overcame life's battering. The concept of Roots of Asia is that of a haven that invites everybody irrelevant of origin and identity. It is a place where anybody will find their home and get some solace. No one gets turned away from here. Every meal served has the essence of emotions: joy, sorrow, anger, or hope. Each visitor leaves a part of their story behind, enriching the soul of the restaurant. This exchange makes Roots of Asia more than just a dining space; it's a haven that fosters an environment filled with unity, love, and the creation of cherished memories that will resound for generations to come.

Our Mission

At Roots of Asia, we try to create a cozy, welcoming environment that respects the many cuisines of Asia through the vegan perspective. Our commitment to inclusivity makes everyone feel welcome, while our quality shines through in every dish. We proudly use the finest plant-based ingredients, sourced from local organic farms and greenhouses. By supporting small businesses around us, we're building a stronger, more sustainable community. Drawing inspiration from recipes passed down through generations, we celebrate the authentic flavors and cherished stories of Asia, delivering comfort and a sense of adventure in every bite.
