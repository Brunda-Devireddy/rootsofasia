Our Preparation Process

Ingredient Sourcing

We believe that the best dishes start with the best ingredients. Our restaurant partners with local organic farms to source fresh, seasonal produce. By working closely with nearby farmers who practice sustainable agriculture, we ensure that every ingredient is grown responsibly and without harmful pesticides.

Additionally, we minimize our carbon footprint by using eco-friendly transportation methods to bring these ingredients from farm to table.

Authentic Cooking Methods

In keeping with our dedication to traditional Asian cuisine, we use time-honored cooking techniques that enhance natural flavors. Techniques such as wok cooking and steaming allow us to create vibrant dishes with minimal processing, keeping each ingredient’s essence intact.

Minimal Waste Ways

Respecting our ingedients means using every part possible. We adopt a minimal waste approach by composting, reusing food scraps for broths, and donating excess food. This reduces waste and aligns with our mission to protect the environment.

Energy-Efficient Kitchen

Our kitchen is equipped with energy-efficient appliances like induction stoves, which allow us to prepare food quickly while saving energy. We’re committed to keeping our ecological impact low by incorporating eco-friendly technology into our cooking process.

Sustainable Packaging

For our carry-out options, we use biodegradable and compostable packaging to ensure that even our to-go meals align with our sustainability values. Our packaging is designed to be environmentally friendly, reducing waste in every part of the dining experience.
